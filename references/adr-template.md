# Architecture Decision Record Template

Create an ADR only for a durable, consequential decision: for example a data store, authorization model, queue, cache, third-party service, deployment target, or public contract. Do not create ADRs for routine implementation details.

Use `context/adr/NNNN-short-title.md`, with sequential zero-padded numbers.

```md
# ADR NNNN: [Decision Title]

## Status
[Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated]

## Date
[YYYY-MM-DD]

## Owners
[Names, team, or role]

## Context
[Problem, constraints, and decision drivers]

## Decision
[The chosen approach and its boundaries]

## Alternatives Considered
- [Alternative]: [why it was not selected]

## Consequences
- Positive: [effects]
- Negative or trade-offs: [effects]
- Follow-up work: [work items]

## Related Records
- Feature specs: [links]
- Issues, tickets, or PRs: [links]
```

Record facts and explicit assumptions. Do not claim that an alternative was evaluated unless the project evidence supports it.
